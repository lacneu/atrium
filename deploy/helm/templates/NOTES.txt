LIFECYCLE — Convex is STATEFUL
   Its SQLite data lives on the PVC and survives pod recreation. Back up with
   `npx convex export` (a volume snapshot is not a substitute). Redeploy the
   stateless tier (frontend/bridge) freely; NEVER delete the PVC unless you
   intend to wipe the database.
